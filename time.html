RESPECT TIME

12/28/2020 Shubh Anand

I don't understand why people want
Everything to go quick
go great
it is same as choosing one of them
If you rush things they won't go well
If things go great they will take time


Making huge goals take huge time
Making small goals take no time
So we should take our time and
Walk up the stairs of goals with
Precautions and take our time


The reason being
One second gone
One minute gone
One hour gone
And so on
None can be bought back
No second
Nither minute
Either hour

So
Once the chance is gone it never comes back
Once the life is gone it never
comes back
